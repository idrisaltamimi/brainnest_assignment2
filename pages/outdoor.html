Running

Running takes me to places where my feet cant. It takes me to places where I can stretch both my legs and gallop till they cry out for mercy. Where I can hear my own breathing and feel every muscle in my body move. Where I can look at different sceneries but at the same time criticize my own form. But most importantly, it takes me to places where everything kind of blurs out and i am left with nothing but my thoughts. It cleanses me, in and out.
